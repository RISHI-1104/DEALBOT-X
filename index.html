The Problem
What keeps shop owners
up at night

63 million small businesses in India run on paper notebooks, memory, and four different UPI apps — every single day.

Udhari chaos
Rs.8,000–22,000 stuck in unpaid customer credit. Tracked in a paper notebook that gets wet and torn.
UPI reconciliation
4 apps, 20–35 minutes every night to manually match payments. One missed payment breaks a customer relationship.
GST terror
Quarterly filing costs Rs.6,000–12,000/year for a CA. English-only portal. Most owners give up entirely.
Inventory blindness
Stock managed by eye. 35% of customers never return after two consecutive stockouts. Dead stock blocks cash.
No customer memory
200 regular customers. Zero data about them. No way to know who stopped coming or who deserves a loyalty reward.
Marketing blackout
Festival offers missed. No Tamil content tools. WhatsApp broadcast fails because customers never saved the number.
The Solution
Everything your shop needs.
Nothing it doesn't.

Built mobile-first, Tamil-first, and offline-first — for the reality of running a small business in India.

Digital Udhari Ledger
Track every credit given, auto-send polite Tamil WhatsApp reminders on day 7 and day 14. Recover 60–70% of pending dues without awkward conversations.
UPI Payment Tracker
Reads UPI SMS from PhonePe, GPay, Paytm and BHIM. Auto-matches each payment to the right customer. Zero manual reconciliation.
Daily P&L in Tamil
Every night: one plain-language sentence. "Inru Rs.4,200 vitreer. Rs.3,400 labam." No accounting jargon. No spreadsheets.
GST Invoice Generator
One tap to generate a GST-compliant invoice with auto-suggested HSN codes. Delivered to customer WhatsApp in 10 seconds. Auto-logged to GSTR-1 draft.
Festival Offer AI
29+ festivals tracked. 3 days before each — auto prompt. One tap generates a Tamil offer image and sends to all your regulars. Rs.2,000–6,000 extra per festival.
Voice Entry in Tamil
Say "Ramesh 450 vaanginaan" — auto-logged to his udhari account in 2 seconds. No typing. Works offline. Kills the paper khaata habit in two weeks.
